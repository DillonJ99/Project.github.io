Welcome to the Phishing Test

Phishing is a type of scam used by cyber criminals to trick people into giving away their personal information, such as their bank or login details. They often do this by creating fake emails or websites that look like they are from a legitimate source, such as a bank. Once the victim enters their information on the fake site, the cyber criminals can use it to steal their money from the real bank.
